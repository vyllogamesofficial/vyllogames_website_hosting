Privacy Policy for Arrows Doom

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Vyllo Games operates the mobile game Arrows Doom. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our game, how it may be used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Arrows Doom, we may collect limited information directly or through third-party services used in the game.

  • Device information, such as device model, operating system, language, and app version
  • Usage information, such as gameplay activity, session length, interactions, and crash data
  • Advertising-related information, such as advertising identifiers and approximate location, as provided by advertising partners
  • Diagnostic information used to detect bugs, improve stability, and prevent abuse

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your full name, home address, or personal messages unless you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve Arrows Doom
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Understand how players use the game
  • Show ads in the game
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Advertising

Arrows Doom may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners, including Google AdMob.

These partners may collect and process certain data, such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, and app interaction data, in order to provide ads, measure performance, and help prevent fraud, depending on your device settings, region, and consent choices.

4. Third-Party Services

Arrows Doom may use third-party services that process data on our behalf or for their own service functions, such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play services
  • Firebase / Unity Analytics / Unity Ads / Crashlytics

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers and partners only as needed to:

  • Serve ads
  • Provide analytics and crash reporting
  • Maintain app functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Children’s Privacy

Arrows Doom is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will review the request.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including maintaining the game, analytics, ad operations, legal compliance, and resolving disputes.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information related to the game and its users. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

Depending on your device, location, and applicable law, you may have choices regarding:

  • Personalized advertising
  • App permissions
  • Resetting your advertising ID
  • Limiting ad tracking through your device settings

If consent is required in your region, it may be requested through the game or through supported advertising consent tools.

10. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, where our service providers or partners operate.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
